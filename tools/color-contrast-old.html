Color Contrast

WCAG 1.4.3 requires text to have adequate contrast so that people who are color blind can comfortably read it. Contrast is measured using a formula that gives a ratio ranging from 1:1 (no contrast) to 21:1 (black & white). Using this formula, the mininum requirements are:

  • 3.0:1 - minimum contrast for large scale text (24px or 20px bold)
  • 4.5:1 - minimum contrast for regular sized text (Level AA)
  • 7.0:1 - enhanced contrast for regular sized text (Level AAA)
